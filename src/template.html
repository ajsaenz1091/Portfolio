About me

My name is Alberto. I come from a small country in Central America called Costa Rica. The primary language there is Spanish, which I grew up speaking. From a very young age, I was able to train my ear to the English language, and practicing along the years I can say with confidence that I have a very solid proficiency.

The acquisition of a new language, made me want to learn more, so I started learning German. Even though I haven't mastered it, I know by experience that it is only a matter of time until I do. Learning a new language is nothing but programming your brain through practice to add a new set of rules and sounds and sometimes even gestures to its repertoire to produce an outcome that will allow you to communicate effectively with other "users" of that specific language. Very similar to programming languages. This is one of the reasons I decided to become a Web Developer. There is also the cognitive aspect of it and the room for creative expression.

Projects

Face Recognition App

This is a simple application that recognizes a face within a picture. The use of it is simple. Just copy the URL from an online picture of a face and paste it in the "detect" bar. Your image will be displayed with a square marking the face. This is possible thanks to the use of an API from Clarifai. You will also see the number of submissions displayed on the screen.

The app is quite simple like I mentioned before, but I hold a huge sense of accomplishment from building it because it was full of challenges that were completely new to me. I came across different problems which were incredible teachers at the end of the road. This project gave me a great deal of confidence with GitHub, beacause it forced me to use it more. It was created using Node.js and Express.js for the server, PostgreSQL for the data base, bcrypt to hash your password so registering is safe, and a set of npm packages.
